MAINTENANCE

Service technicians at SAW are not merely trained to replace service parts; they employ the country’s leading motoring personnel and are able to offer you comprehensive advice on maintenance matters while also being able to extract reliable horsepower from your vehicle. Backed by the fact that SAW is the only aftermarket workshop group in the country that has a dedicated research, development and training centre, will give you the assurance that you will not only be assisted by some of the best well trained staff in the industry, but also that the quality of workmanship and specification level rendered, remains of the highest order regardless of the branch you visit.

Strand Auto Workshop (SAW) is a leader in the field of automotive servicing, repair and maintenance of nearly all diesel bakkies and petrol vehicles. With more than 35 years of automotive maintenance experience; backed by the Automobile Association Quality Assurance (AAQA), the Retail Motor Industry (RMI) and our own unique SAC “Why Worry Warranty”, we should surely be your first choice when it comes to small, medium and large commercial vehicle fleet maintenance. Not only do we take care of service, repair and maintenance issues, we hold in high regard the importance of preventative maintenance y have a significant financial benefit for company.
