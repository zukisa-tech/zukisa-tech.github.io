About SAW

SAW is Auto Workshop found by Sibusiso Mthembu and Tony Grey in 1996. They started in garage at tony house. Since establishment the SAW has been working with quite a lot of well established motor companies such SNM, BENZ, DMG etc. SAW has been awarded 10 times for providing best services to it's customers. At SAW we put our customers first, we believe working together we can achieve and we believe each and every car in our premises are equal regarding the worth or model of the car.

SAW provide variety of services from selling car parts, tyres, fix cars and has became well second car shop. We are in partnership with lot of brands such as BMW, BENZ, VW, JEEP, Land Rover etc. We are in partnership with importing companies, we import parts from Europe, China, America, Germany etc. Our customers comes first hence we established this partnership.

OUR MISSION

At SAW our mission is reach our customers across the country and provide the best service. Reaching our customers is the first priority but the goal is to become a one stop workshop where you will get any car part you need from any brand and have an option to trade in your car for upgrade. We aim to esta partnership with auctioning companies.
